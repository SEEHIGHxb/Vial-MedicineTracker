How to Sync Samsung Smart Watch

1

Standard Browser Prompts: Grant notification permissions when prompted. Standard web notifications are triggered directly by your desktop or mobile browser.

2

For Windows (Phone Link): Connect your Samsung/Android phone using the Link to Windows application. Standard notifications on your PC will mirror to your phone.

3

Galaxy Wearable Sync: Open the Galaxy Wearable app on your phone, go to **Watch settings > Notifications**, and ensure notification forwarding is enabled for your phone's browser or Phone Link app.
